My Projects

Image Recognition with Convolutional Neural Networks

Leveraging the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), this image recognition system can classify images into 1000 different categories with high accuracy. Trained on the ImageNet dataset, the model demonstrates the efficacy of deep learning in computer vision tasks.

Chatbot Development using Sequence-to-Sequence Models

This project involves creating a conversational agent using a sequence-to-sequence model with attention mechanisms. The chatbot is trained on a dataset of dialogues to perform tasks ranging from customer service to engaging in small talk, showcasing the versatility of neural network architectures in natural language processing.

Drug Discovery with Generative Adversarial Networks

Applying Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to the field of medicinal chemistry, this project generates novel molecular structures for drug discovery. The model learns from known pharmacologically active compounds to propose new candidates for drug development pipelines.
